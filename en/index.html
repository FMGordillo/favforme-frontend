Frequent Questions

Welcome! Let's answer some useful questions so you can get started doing favors.

What is FavForMe?

It is a social network centered on exchanging services with a positive outlook. Favors are the currency of true connections, the most effective way to establish a lasting bond and expand your personal and professional contacts. In FavForMe all favors are important: from those published by private users to the campaigns carried out by Non-profit organizations and companies.

Why Register?

To participate in FavForMe you must be registered. It is a very simple procedure! You can register as a private user or as an entity.

Is it Free?

Yes, FavForMe is free for you to start right now!

How do I register?

Download our app in Google Play and sign up now!

Is there a minimum age to register with FavForMe?

 Yes, for legal reasons, the minimum age to register is 18 years.

¿Cómo hago un favor en FavForMe?

In the Wall section of FavForMe you will be able to see the FAVORS REQUESTED AND OFFERED.
If you think you can complete a favor do not hesitate to sign up, someone is waiting for you!

How do I ask or offer a favor?

From the Wall section, click on "WHAT DO YOU NEED NOW?" or WHAT DO YOU HAVE TO OFFER NOW ?. In your publication you need to explain what it is that you need or want to offer. Try to take some time to write your publication as clear as possible. You can add pictures and comments to make your post more attractive; remember your explanation effects how other users interact with the favor

Is it important to perform the favor with the greatest possible effort?

Yes, it is very important because, after the favor has been performed the users who participated will have the opportunity to qualify each other.

What are Coins?

 Coins are the currency that FavForMe invented to compensate users for getting out there and making true connections. For each favor performed users will receive coins. Coins are needed to request favors.

How do I get coins?
Hay 3 formas de conseguir Monedas para los usuarios:
  1. 1. Performing a favor for another user.
  2. Performing a favor for a Non-Profit Organization or Company.
  3. Completing Achievements, example: If you invite 5 friends you will get a coin as a reward.
